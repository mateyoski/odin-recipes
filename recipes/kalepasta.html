How to make kale pasta

Kale pasta is something I have only recently tried and got really surprised by how well it fits into a pasta. All you need is a couple simple ingredients, truly, making it a solid option for a dinner, especially when you dont have much time on your hands.

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (any kind)
  • 200g kale
  • 100g gorgonzola cheese
  • 2 medium white onions
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cloves of garlic

  1. Boil a pot of water, salted generously
  2. Cut the onions into small cubes
  3. Cut the hard stems from kale and wash it thoroughly
  4. Put the pasta into the pot when it starts boiling
  5. Mince the garlic
  6. Heat a pan on medium heat
  7. Put the onions and garlic until it releases fragrance and softens up
  8. Put the kale into the pan
  9. Stir until it softens
  10. Add gorgonzola cheese into the pan
  11. Make sure all of it is melted
  12. Reserve 100ml of pasta water and drain the rest of the pasta
  13. Put the pasta into the pan
  14. Pour the pasta water into the pan, stirring until everything is homogenised
  15. Serve on a plate with
